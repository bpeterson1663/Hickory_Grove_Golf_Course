The Golf Course

Hickory Grove Golf Course is a nine-hole course located in the heart of Southwest Wisconsin. Located just a few miles west of Fennimore, Hickory Grove offers an incredible golf experience from the 1st tee to the 9th green. You will be blown away by the well-kept condition of the course and beautiful scenery surrounding the course.

Rates

Hickory Grove offers great golf at a reasonable price. Wether you're looking to become a member or just paying for a quick nine, you will find our prices are the best around. We also has a reciprocal agreement with the following courses: House On The Rock Resort, Lancaster, Viroqua, Darlington, Cole Acres, Dodge Point, Deer Valley, Prairie Du Chien, and Platteville.

Practice Facilities

Want to warm up before your round? Have some free time during your lunch break? Well then come out to the driving range to hit a few. Our range offers both mats and natural grass surface to hit off of. We also have a practice green to help you adjust to the speed of our greens before your round.
